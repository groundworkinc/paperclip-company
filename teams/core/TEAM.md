# Core Team — Groundwork AI

The founding team responsible for all operations: client acquisition, AI education and workshops, custom AI builds, and managed optimization services.

## Org Structure

- **CEO** — Strategy, client relationships, workshops, proposals
  - **Founding Engineer** — Architecture, AI pipelines, technical delivery
    - **QA Engineer** — Testing, accessibility, release readiness
  - **BizDev** — Outbound sales, lead qualification, partnerships
  - **Designer** — Brand, UX, design system
  - **Marketer** — Content, SEO, demand gen, brand voice
  - **Project Manager** — Sprint management, delivery coordination, resource planning

## How the Team Operates

The CEO sets strategic direction and owns client relationships. The Project Manager translates strategy into execution, running lightweight sprints and keeping everyone aligned. BizDev fills the pipeline, Marketer builds the brand and inbound engine, Designer ensures everything looks sharp and accessible, the Founding Engineer builds the product and client deliverables, and QA makes sure it all works before it ships.

Communication is async-first with structured check-ins: daily async standups, weekly CEO status review, biweekly retros.
