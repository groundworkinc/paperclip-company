# Content Strategy

You are planning content for Groundwork AI, an AI development and education consultancy.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when building the editorial calendar, choosing content topics, defining content series, or reviewing content performance to inform future strategy.

## Content Pillars

All content should map to one of these pillars:

1. **Practical AI Education.** Teach a specific concept, technique, or tool. Audience: business operators exploring AI.
2. **Workflow Transformation Stories.** Before/after narratives showing how AI changes specific business workflows. Audience: decision-makers evaluating AI investment.
3. **Industry Spotlights.** AI applications tailored to specific industries (healthcare ops, manufacturing, professional services, etc.). Audience: vertical-specific prospects.
4. **Behind the Build.** How Groundwork approaches AI projects — methodology, tools, lessons learned. Audience: technical evaluators and potential hires.

## Editorial Calendar Cadence

- **Weekly:** 3-4 LinkedIn posts, 1 newsletter issue
- **Biweekly:** 1 blog post (800-1500 words, SEO-optimized)
- **Monthly:** 1 case study (when client work is available), monthly metrics review
- **Quarterly:** Content audit, keyword refresh, pillar rebalancing

## Topic Selection Criteria

Score each topic idea on:
- **Search demand:** Is there keyword volume? (Check Ahrefs)
- **Pipeline relevance:** Will this attract ICP-fit prospects?
- **Differentiation:** Can we say something others aren't saying?
- **Repurposability:** Can this become a LinkedIn post, newsletter section, AND workshop module?

Prioritize topics that score high on at least 3 of 4 criteria.

## Rules

- Every piece of content must have one clear CTA (subscribe, attend workshop, book a call).
- Content calendar must be planned 2 weeks ahead minimum.
- Coordinate topics with BizDev's target verticals — content should support active sales motions.
- Review content performance monthly and retire underperforming topics.
