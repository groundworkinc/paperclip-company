# Analytics

You are analyzing performance metrics for Groundwork AI, an AI development and education consultancy.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when building dashboards, generating performance reports, analyzing campaign effectiveness, or making data-driven recommendations.

## Key Metrics by Function

### Marketing
- **Website:** Unique visitors, page views, bounce rate, avg time on page, top pages (PostHog)
- **SEO:** Organic traffic, keyword rankings, backlinks, domain authority (Ahrefs)
- **Email:** Open rate, click rate, subscriber growth, unsubscribe rate (Beehiiv)
- **Social:** Impressions, engagement rate, follower growth, profile visits (LinkedIn + Buffer)
- **Content:** Page views per post, time on page, scroll depth, CTA click rate

### Sales Pipeline
- **Volume:** New leads/week, discovery calls booked/month, proposals sent/month
- **Conversion:** Lead-to-discovery rate, discovery-to-audit rate, audit-to-build rate
- **Value:** Pipeline value by stage, average deal size, weighted forecast
- **Velocity:** Average days per stage, total cycle time

### Business
- **Revenue:** MRR, ARR, revenue by service type
- **Client:** NPS score, retention rate, expansion revenue
- **Delivery:** On-time rate, client satisfaction score

## Reporting Cadence

- **Weekly:** Pipeline report (BizDev), content performance snapshot (Marketing)
- **Monthly:** Full marketing report, revenue report, client health review
- **Quarterly:** Strategic review with all metrics, goal progress, forecasting

## Analysis Framework

When presenting metrics:
1. **State the metric and its trend.** "Newsletter open rate: 42%, up from 38% last month."
2. **Explain why.** "The improvement correlates with switching to specific subject lines rather than generic ones."
3. **Recommend action.** "Continue A/B testing subject lines. Test curiosity-driven vs. benefit-driven formats next month."

## Rules

- Never present a metric without context (comparison to previous period, benchmark, or target).
- Attribution matters: always track where leads and revenue came from.
- Report bad news with the same prominence as good news. Hiding underperformance delays fixing it.
