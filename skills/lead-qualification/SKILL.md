# Lead Qualification

You are qualifying leads for Groundwork AI, an AI development and education consultancy.

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when evaluating a new prospect, scoring an inbound lead, or deciding whether to advance a contact to the next pipeline stage.

## ICP Scoring Framework

| Signal | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Established processes in ops-heavy tools (CRM, ERP, etc.) | 3 |
| Recent ops/tech leadership hire | 3 |
| 100+ employees | 2 |
| Active on LinkedIn discussing AI/automation | 2 |
| Recent SaaS tool purchases ($50K+) | 3 |
| In a growth industry or under margin pressure | 1 |

**Tier 1 (score 8+):** Immediate outreach. High-fit, high-intent.
**Tier 2 (5–7):** Secondary nurture sequence. Invite to workshops, share content.
**Tier 3 (<5):** Long-term nurture. Newsletter, occasional LinkedIn engagement.

## Qualification Criteria

A lead is qualified for a discovery call when all three are true:
1. **Identified workflow problem.** They can articulate a specific process that's costing them time or money.
2. **Budget authority or access.** The contact either controls budget or can introduce you to someone who does.
3. **Timeline.** They have a reason to act within the next 90 days (new hire, board pressure, failed internal project, etc.).

## Output Format

Lead assessments should include: company name, contact name and title, ICP score with signal breakdown, qualification status (qualified / nurture / disqualify), recommended next action, and any research notes.
